overview

The Adventures of Hatim follows the journey of Hatim, a young prince who sets
out to save the world from the evil sorcerer Zargam. Along the way, he
encounters various mythical creatures, battles powerful demons, and solves
challenging riddles.

Cast

  • Hatim - Played by Rajbeer Singh
  • Zargam - Played by Nirmal Pandey
  • Perizaad - Played by Pooja Banerjee
  • Raqesh - Played by Kiku Sharda

Key Information

    Genre: Fantasy, Adventure
    Original Release: 2003 - 2004
    Creator: Sagar Films
    Language: Hindi
    Number of Seasons: 1
    Number of Episodes: 47

Characters

    Hatim: The brave and noble prince on a quest to vanquish evil.
    Perizaad: Hatim's loyal and magical companion, often assisting him on his journey.
    Zargam: The powerful sorcerer who seeks to conquer the world.
    Haseena: Zargam's cunning and treacherous accomplice.
