Recipe of Kashmiri tea

A traditional Kashmiri tea known for its pink colour,
tasty and sugar makes it,s taste double and garnish with almonds makes its look lovely.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of water
  • 4 tablespoons fennel
  • 4 cups milk
  • 3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 6-7 green cardamom pods, crushed
  • 1-2 cinnamon stick
  • 5 to 6 sticks majeet(for naturally pink colour)
  • 1/3 cup almonds, blanched and sliced
  • 1/3 cup pistachios, chopped
  • Dried rose petals for garnish(optional)

Method

  1. Boil water in a pot and add fennel ,cardamom,cinnamon stick,and majeet sticks
  2. Let it boiled for 4-6 minutes until it changes it,s colour
  3. Add milk to the pot and bring it to a boil,then reduce heat to low.
  4. Add sugar and heat or boiled for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Remove from heat and strain the tea into cups or a teapot.
  6. Garnish with sliced almonds, chopped pistachios.
  7. Serve hot with your favourite snacks or cookies.
