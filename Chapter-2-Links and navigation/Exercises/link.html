Overview
"Hatim" is an Indian fantasy television series that originally aired from 2003 to 2004. It follows the adventures of Hatim, a young prince who sets out on a journey to defeat the evil sorcerer Zargam and save the world from his tyranny. Along the way, Hatim encounters various mythical creatures, magical beings, and formidable challenges.
